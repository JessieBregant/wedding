700 East Adams Street
Springfield, Illinois

We have secured a special group rate for rooms at the Wyndham Springfield City Centre. Formerly a Hilton hotel, the updated Wyndham is just a few short blocks from the Sangamo Club. The Wyndham is also just a short walk from all of the downtown Lincoln sites, including the Abraham Lincoln Presidential Library and Museum. Getting to Springfield

Abraham Lincoln Capital Airport in Springfield is served by American Airlines, United Airlines, and Allegiant Air. Nearby Central Illinois Regional Airport in Bloomington-Normal is also served by Delta. St. Louis is about 1.5 hours from Springfield by car, and Chicago (O'Hare and Midway airports) are approximately 3.5-4 hours away. Springfield can also be reached by train from Chicago and St. Louis!
